This repository documents the research and development work carried out during my **Professional Internship** at the Interdisciplinary Center for Research and Teaching of Science (**CIIEC**) of the Benem茅rita Universidad Aut贸noma de Puebla (**BUAP**).

The work focuses on the intersection of three key areas: **Machine Learning, High-Energy Physics, and Quantum Computing**.

##  Main Projects
The repository is divided into three fundamental axes:
### 1. Artificial Neural Networks (ANN)
Introduction to classical Deep Learning.
- **Objective:** Handwritten digit classification.
- **Dataset:** MNIST.
- **Technology:** Implementation of basic architectures (Perceptron and CNN).

### 2. Quantum Computing and Cryptography
Study of fundamental quantum algorithms and information security.
- **Shor's Algorithm:** Implementation and simulation for integer factorization.
- **RSA Cryptography:** Analysis of vulnerabilities against quantum attacks.
- **Tools:** Circuit simulation using **IBM Qiskit**.

### 3. Google Summer of Code (GSoC) 2025 - ML4SCI
Development of evaluation tasks for the organization *Machine Learning for Science*.
- **Graph Neural Networks (GNN):** Jet Classification (Quark vs. Gluon) using geometric architectures (MPNN and GAT).
- **Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (KAN):** Implementation of classical and hybrid KAN architectures, and theoretical proposal of a **Quantum KAN (Q-KAN)**.
